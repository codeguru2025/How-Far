# Privacy Policy

**How Far - Ride Sharing Application**

**Last Updated: December 11, 2025**

---

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to How Far ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use the Service.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you register for an account, we collect:
- **Phone Number**: Required for account creation and verification
- **Name**: First name and optionally last name for identification
- **Password**: Securely hashed and stored for account access
- **Profile Photo**: Optional, for driver and rider identification

### 2.2 Payment Information

We collect payment-related information including:
- **Mobile Money Account Details**: For EcoCash, OneMoney, and InnBucks transactions
- **Transaction History**: Records of top-ups, payments, and withdrawals
- **Wallet Balance**: Your current account balance within the app

**Note**: We do not store complete mobile money credentials. Payments are processed through PayNow, a secure third-party payment gateway.

### 2.3 Location Information

We collect location data to provide our core ride-sharing service:
- **Precise Location**: GPS coordinates when you're actively using the app
- **Trip Routes**: Origin and destination points for ride matching
- **Driver Location**: Real-time tracking during active trips (for drivers)

### 2.4 Device Information

We automatically collect:
- **Device Identifier**: Unique device ID for security purposes
- **Device Type**: Phone model and operating system version
- **App Version**: To ensure compatibility and support

### 2.5 Usage Data

We collect information about how you use the Service:
- **Trip History**: Records of completed rides
- **Search Queries**: Destinations you've searched for
- **Booking Activity**: Seats booked, cancellations, and QR scans

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

### 3.1 Provide Core Services
- Match riders with drivers going in the same direction
- Process payments between riders and drivers
- Enable real-time trip tracking
- Generate QR codes for secure payments

### 3.2 Improve and Personalize the Service
- Optimize route matching algorithms
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Provide personalized fare estimates

### 3.3 Safety and Security
- Verify user identities
- Prevent fraudulent transactions
- Detect and prevent abuse of the platform
- Respond to safety incidents

### 3.4 Communications
- Send ride confirmations and updates
- Notify you of booking status changes
- Send important service announcements
- Provide customer support

### 3.5 Legal Compliance
- Comply with applicable laws and regulations
- Respond to legal requests and prevent harm
- Enforce our Terms of Service

## 4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 With Other Users
- **Riders see**: Driver's name, rating, vehicle details, and current location during a trip
- **Drivers see**: Rider's name, pickup/dropoff locations, and booking details

### 4.2 With Service Providers
We share information with trusted third parties who assist in providing the Service:
- **PayNow**: Payment processing (Harare, Zimbabwe)
- **Supabase**: Database and authentication services
- **Google Maps**: Location and mapping services
- **Expo/EAS**: App distribution and updates

### 4.3 For Legal Reasons
We may disclose information:
- To comply with legal obligations
- To protect our rights or the safety of users
- In response to lawful requests by public authorities
- In connection with legal proceedings

### 4.4 Business Transfers
If How Far is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:
- Provide the Service to you
- Comply with legal obligations
- Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

**Retention Periods**:
- Account data: Until account deletion, plus 2 years for legal compliance
- Trip history: 3 years from trip completion
- Transaction records: 7 years (financial regulations)
- Location data: 90 days after trip completion

## 6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:
- **Encryption**: Data encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest
- **Password Security**: Passwords are cryptographically hashed
- **Access Controls**: Limited access to personal data
- **Regular Audits**: Security reviews and updates

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
