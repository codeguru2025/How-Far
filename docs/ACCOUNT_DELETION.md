# How Far - Account & Data Deletion Request

## Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your How Far account and associated data, please follow these steps:

### Option 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)
1. Open the How Far app
2. Go to **Profile** → **Settings**
3. Scroll down and tap **Delete Account**
4. Confirm your request
5. Your account will be permanently deleted within 7 days

### Option 2: Email Request
If you cannot access the app, email us at:

**Email:** support@howfar.app

**Subject:** Account Deletion Request

**Include:**
- Your registered phone number
- Reason for deletion (optional)

We will process your request within 7 business days.

---

## Data That Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is **permanently removed**:

| Data Type | Deletion Status |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Profile information (name, phone, email) | ✅ Deleted |
| Wallet balance and transaction history | ✅ Deleted |
| Ride/trip history | ✅ Deleted |
| Driver registration and vehicle info | ✅ Deleted |
| Chat messages | ✅ Deleted |
| Saved payment methods | ✅ Deleted |
| Profile photos | ✅ Deleted |

---

## Data Retained for Legal/Business Purposes

Some data may be retained for a limited period as required by law:

| Data Type | Retention Period | Reason |
|-----------|-----------------|--------|
| Transaction records | 7 years | Tax and financial regulations |
| Legal dispute records | Until resolved | Legal requirements |

This retained data is anonymized and cannot be linked back to your identity.

---

## Processing Time

- **Standard requests:** 7 business days
- **Complex cases:** Up to 30 days

You will receive confirmation once your account is deleted.
