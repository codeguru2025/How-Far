How Far is a peer-to-peer ride-sharing platform that connects drivers traveling on specific routes with riders seeking transportation along those routes. The Service includes:

- **For Riders**: Finding available trips, booking seats, and making payments via QR code

**Important**: How Far is a technology platform that facilitates connections between riders and drivers. We do not provide transportation services directly. Drivers are independent operators, not our employees.

## 6. Payments and Fees

### 6.1 Payment System
How Far uses an in-app wallet system for all transactions. Payments are processed through PayNow, supporting:
- EcoCash
- OneMoney
- InnBucks
- Bank transfers

### 6.2 Fee Structure
- **Rider Fee**: 2.5% of the fare, charged to riders on top of the base fare
- **Driver Fee**: 7.5% of the fare, deducted from driver earnings
- **Additional Fees**: Custom pickup or dropoff locations may incur additional charges set by drivers

### 6.3 Fares
Drivers set fares for their trips. Riders see the total cost before booking, including all fees.

### 6.4 QR Code Payments
Payment is completed when the driver scans the rider's QR code. Once scanned:
- Funds are transferred from the rider's wallet
- The driver receives payment (minus the driver fee)
- The transaction is final

### 6.5 Refunds
Refunds may be issued at our discretion for:
- Technical errors causing incorrect charges
- Trips not completed due to driver cancellation
- Duplicate charges

Refunds are NOT provided for:
- Rider no-shows after booking
- Rider cancellations after trip commencement
- Dissatisfaction with route chosen by driver

### 6.6 Withdrawals
Drivers may withdraw earnings to their mobile money accounts. Withdrawals are processed within 24-48 hours during business days.

## 7. Trips and Bookings

### 7.1 Creating Trips (Drivers)
Drivers may create trips by specifying:
- Origin and destination
- Available seats
- Base fare per seat
- Optional waypoints and variable pricing

### 7.2 Booking Seats (Riders)
Riders may search for and book seats on available trips. Bookings require:
- Sufficient wallet balance (fare + rider fee)
- Acceptance by the driver (for pending bookings)

### 7.3 Booking Confirmation
- **Pending**: Awaiting driver confirmation
- **Confirmed**: Driver has accepted; rider should show up at pickup point
- **Completed**: Trip finished and payment processed

### 7.4 Cancellations
**Riders** may cancel bookings:
- Before driver confirmation: No penalty
- After confirmation: Subject to cancellation policy

**Drivers** may cancel trips:
- All confirmed riders will be notified
- Repeated cancellations may affect driver rating and eligibility
